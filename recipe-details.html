Arugula Cheese Grinder with Basil Mayonnaise

This sandwich can be made with almost any combination of bread and cheese. French bread and Havarti cheese are ideal though.

Time
13 min
Servings
04
Nutrition
300 cal

Directions

Combine the first 4 ingredients. Cut the bread loaves in half horizontally; spread the mayonnaise evenly over the cut slices of bread.
Arrange 2 tomato slices over the bottom half of each loaf and top with 3 tablespoons of alfalfa sprouts, 1 cheese slice, and 5 arugula leaves.
Top with remaining bread halves and serve immediately.
